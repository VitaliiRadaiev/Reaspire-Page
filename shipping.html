Shipping & Returns

Package Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email notification with a tracking number. You can also view tracking information if you have a raespira.com account. Log in to My Account and select Order History & Tracking to find out the status of your order. If your order has not arrived within the estimated delivery schedule above, please contact our Customer Experience team on info@raespira.com.

Duties & Taxes

For International orders, shipping fees are exclusive of duties and taxes. These charges are levied by the country you are shipping to, and payment is necessary to release your order from customs on arrival. If the customs charges are not paid COD (cash on delivery), the order will be returned back to Raespira.

Missing or Damaged Product

Did your package get damaged while its transportation? Please contact our Customer Experience team by email at info@raespira.com for assistance with missing or damaged items.

Return Policy

Our commitment is to provide the highest quality 100% natural products in the world. And we think you’ll love our products as much as we love making them. However, if you’re not 100% happy with your choice, let us know. All products can be returned or exchanged within 14 days of purchase. Discounted items are eligible for an exchange and we can offer you money return only. Money return always equals your purchase price, regardless future product price difference. Simply contact our Customer Experience team by email at info@raespira.com
