KINTSUGI is the Japanese art of putting broken pottery pieces back together with gold. Every break is unique, and the 400-year-old technique highlights the "scars" as a part of the design. The repairs and scars are visible — yet somehow beautiful.

EVERY SCAR TELLS A STORY and the ritual of Kintsugi teaches us an important lesson: In the process of repairing & healing what have got broken, we actually create something more unique, beautiful and resilient. We all have scars and stories, which made us who we are today.
